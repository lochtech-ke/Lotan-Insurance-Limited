Our Four Core Products

Engineered to solve specific capital constraints and structural challenges.

1. Credit Protection Policy

Loan Enablement & Capital Unlocking Instrument

Unlocks lending capacity and reduces capital costs by providing high-integrity credit protection. It ensures an outstanding loan is fully repaid in the event of the borrower's death, permanent disability, or involuntary job loss.

  • For Lenders: Mitigates credit risk, reduces NPLs, enables confident lending.
  • For Borrowers: Protects assets, avoids debt burden on families.

2. Performance Security Bonds

Project Financing & Capital Unlocking

Minimizes cash-trapped collateral, freeing up working capital for reinvestment into project delivery. Provides critical financial security assuring employers that the contractor will fulfill all contract terms.

  • Protects project owners from catastrophic financial loss.
  • Differentiates contractors by demonstrating true institutional stability.

3. Advance Payment Guarantees

Mobilization Capital Enablement

Transforms standard requirements into mobilization capital engines, ensuring immediate liquidity for rapid start-ups without exposing project owners to upfront cash risk.

  • Eliminates risk associated with releasing large sums of money upfront.
  • Enables contractors to secure working capital for equipment and materials.

4. Bid Bonds

Competitive Bidding Capital

Allows contractors to demonstrate financial credibility and participate in competitive, capital-intensive opportunities—without jeopardizing internal liquidity reserves.

  • Assures the project owner that the bidder is financially capable.
  • Reduces procurement risk and prevents costly re-tendering scenarios.

Credit Protection Insurance Policy Technical Summary

Sentinel Insure (the “Company”) undertakes to assume and absorb the credit risk under the Insured Loan, paying the benefits set out in the schedule subject to the terms and exclusions below.

1. Keyman Death Cover

Pays the amount advanced or outstanding balance. Claims advising required within 60 days. Required docs: Death Cert, Loan Statement, Schedule of Repayments, Police Report (if accidental).

2. Total Permanent Disability (TPD)

Lump sum payment of outstanding loan if Keyman is permanently unable to work. 3-month waiting period (waived for accidents). Requires recognized medical certification within 30 days of assessment.

3. Total Temporary Disability (TTD)

Covers a maximum of 6 monthly installments. Minimum 30 days off work constraint. Automatic 6-month reinstatement applied post-claim.

4. Fidelity Guarantee & 5. Cybercrime

Fidelity protects from losses caused by employee embezzlement or theft. Cybercrime secures against network extortion, ransomware, network security liability, and data breach responses.

6. Business Interruption

Protects missing net income and covers fixed costs (payroll, rent) and extra expenses when operations are disrupted by physical loss, contingent supply chain blocks, or civil authority actions.

7. Cash-in-Transit

On-premises theft, vault burglary, transit cash losses, or hold-up loss reimbursement.

8. General Conditions & Exclusions

Exclusions apply for intentional self-injury in the first 2 years, acts of war, pre-existing physical infirmity knowledge, alcoholic intemperance, extreme power sports, and non-scheduled aviation.

9. Premium Payment & Refund

Calculated per unit rate (1.8% standard) paid in lump sum in advance. Pro-rata premium refund guarantees available on early settlement, sans administrative/tax deductions. This is a true risk premium, carrying zero surrender value at maturity.

Jurisdiction: Governed by the laws of the Republic of South Africa. Subject to Cession conditions (irrevocable ceding to financier as security).
