Credit Protection Insurance Policy

Master Policy Document by Sentinel Insure. Features, benefits, and technical scope of cover designed to assume credit risk under the Insured Loan.

1. Keyman Death Cover

Pays the amount advanced or outstanding loan balance at the time of claim. The Company must be advised within 60 days. Requires Death Certificate, Loan Statement showing balance, Schedule of Repayments, Loan Acceptance Letter, and a Police Report (if accidental).

2. Total Permanent Disability (TPD)

Lump sum payment of outstanding loan if the Keyman is permanently unable to work. 3-month waiting/deferred period (waived for accidents). Requires certification by a Sentinel Insure recognized medical practitioner. Must be advised within 30 days of assessment.

3. Total Temporary Disability (TTD)

Covers up to six (6) monthly loan repayment installments if unfit to work for a minimum of 30 days. After payout, the insured is temporarily off TTD cover for 6 months, reinstating automatically thereafter.

4. Fidelity Guarantee

Protects businesses from financial losses caused by dishonest acts of employees, including loss of money, embezzlement, theft, forgery, and misappropriation of company funds.

5. Cybercrime

Protects against digital threats. Covers Data Breach Responses (notification, PR), Ransomware/Extortion expenses, Cyber-related Business Interruption, Forensic Investigations, and Network Security Liability to third parties.

6. Business Interruption

Covers Lost Net Income, Fixed Costs (rent, payroll), Extra Expenses (temporary space), Civil Authority access denial, and Contingent loss from supplier chain disruptions. Includes Extended Period of Indemnity.

7. Cash-in-Transit & Cash-on-Hand

Reimburses for loss of cash via On-Premises Theft, Off-Premises Transit loss (armored/employee transit), Safe/Vault Burglary, Robbery & Hold-Up, and optional Employee Dishonesty Extension.

8. General Conditions & Exclusions

No payment resulting from:

  • Intentional self-injury/suicide in first 2 years.
  • Acts of war, terrorism, riot.
  • Pre-existing physical infirmity known prior to entry (applies first 6 months).
  • Alcoholic intemperance and unprescribed drugs.
  • Mountaineering, power racing, fighting (unless self defense).
  • Aviation other than fare-paying scheduled passenger.

9. Premium Payment & Refund

Current rate is 1.8% of total loan amount, paid in a lump sum in advance. Pro-rata refund available on early settlement (less administrative costs). This is a true risk premium generating no surrender value.

10. Legal, Termination & Force Majeure

Governed by Republic of South Africa laws. Irrevocable Cession of rights to financier as security. 60 days cancellation notice required by either party. Force Majeure applies to epidemics, strikes, or acts of God continuing >30 days.
