Spotlight on Key Financial Solutions: Credit Life Insurance
What It Is
A specialized policy that ensures an outstanding loan is fully repaid in the event of the borrower's death, permanent disability, or involuntary job loss.
How It Works: Protecting All Parties
Borrower Secures Loan: Credit Life Insurance is linked to a specific loan (mortgage financing, bank loans, asset financing, microfinance lending etc.) taken by the borrower.
Insurance Activation: In the event of the borrower's death, disability, or incapacitation, the insurance policy activates.
Lender Receives Payment: The lender is the beneficiary, receiving direct payment from the insurer to settle the outstanding debt.
Coverage Adjusts: The insurance coverage decreases proportionally as the borrower repays the loan over time.
Value Proposition
For Lenders (Banks, MFIs): Mitigates the risk of loan defaults, protects the loan portfolio, and ensures business continuity by transferring default risk to a stable insurer.
For Borrowers: Provides profound peace of mind, ensuring their family or business partners are not burdened by debt during a time of crisis and that key assets (like a home) are preserved.

Credit Protection Policy: Comprehensive Coverage
Our Credit Protection Policy is a comprehensive insurance solution that protects both lenders and borrowers by ensuring loan obligations are met even in unforeseen circumstances.
KEY COVERAGE AREAS WITH CLIENT BENEFITS
Keyman Death Cover
Pays the outstanding loan balance upon the death of the borrower. Benefit equals the outstanding loan balance at time of claim. Claims must be reported within 60 days of death.
CLIENT BENEFITS: Protects the lender's portfolio from default risk. Protects the borrower's family from inheriting debt burden. Preserves family assets pledged as collateral.
Total Permanent Disability (TPD) Cover
Pays outstanding loan balance when borrower becomes permanently unable to work. Covers injury or illness preventing work in any suitable occupation. 3-month waiting period (waived for accidents). Lump sum payment of outstanding loan amount. Medical certification required from recognized practitioner.
Total Temporary Disability (TTD) Cover
Covers temporary inability to work for minimum 30 days. Pays up to 6 monthly loan installments during disability period. 3-month waiting period (waived for accidents). Medical certification required. Automatic reinstatement after 6-month benefit period.

Additional Protections
Fidelity Guarantee: Protection against employee dishonesty and embezzlement.
Cybercrime Coverage: Protection from data breaches, ransomware, and cyber attacks.
Business Interruption: Coverage for lost income due to operational disruptions.
Cash-in-Transit & Cash-on-Hand: Protection for cash losses during storage or transport.

PREMIUM & TERMS
Premium Rate: 1.8% of total loan amount (standard rate).
Payment: Lump sum in advance on loan amount.
Duration: Matches loan repayment term.

REFUND CLAUSE - PREMIUM PAYMENT AND REFUND
Early Settlement of the Loan: If the borrower settles the loan before the agreed repayment period ends, a refund is available.
Insurer Suspects Foul Play: If the insurer identifies fraudulent activity or misrepresentation.
Contract Termination Before Loan Disbursement: If the insured decides to terminate the contract with the financier before the loan is disbursed.
Important Refund Terms: Refunds are processed less administrative costs and taxes as per the insurance act. Premium paid is a true risk premium and the policy will not acquire a surrender value at the end of the loan period.

Performance Security Bonds & Advance Payment Guarantees: Project Protection Solutions
Performance Security Bonds
What It Is
A guarantee (surety bond) provided by Lotan on behalf of a contractor (the Principal) to a project owner (the Obligee). It assures the owner that the contractor will fulfill all terms of the contract.
How It Works
If the contractor defaults or fails to perform, the project owner can claim against the bond to recover financial losses up to the bond amount (typically 10% of the contract sum).

Advance Payment Guarantee
What It Is
A guarantee issued by Lotan to protect an employer's advance payment made to a contractor, typically for large projects requiring upfront capital for materials or mobilization.

Bid Bonds: Securing Your Competitive Advantage
A Bid Bond is a guarantee issued by Lotan Insurance on behalf of a contractor to a project owner, ensuring that the contractor will enter into a contract at the bid price if selected. It protects project owners from contractors who bid but fail to execute.

GENERAL CONDITIONS AND POLICY EXCLUSIONS
Sentinel Insure is not obliged to make any payment in respect of any condition or event arising directly or indirectly from or traceable to:
8.1 Intentional self-injury or attempted suicide, or self-injury during the first two years of entry date (whether sane or insane).
8.2 Any act of war: Civil war, Insurrection, Riot, commotion, Act of terrorism or other act of violence, military action.
8.3 Any physical defect or infirmity of which Keyman was aware and which has its origin prior to the granting of life insurance to the Keyman. This clause shall apply in the first 6 months of every individual policy.
8.4 Alcoholic intemperance and use of drugs even when prescribed by qualified doctor.
8.5 Actively participating in mountaineering, horse riding, hunting, motor racing, power boat racing or fighting (except in self defence).
